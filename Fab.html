FABULOUS CREATURES

Characters

Sasha - An influencer - Arrogant and Rude
Jasmine – An Influencer - Arrogant and Rude
Skye – Tv host - Sweet and efficient
Jo Jones – Make-up Artist – Charming and helpful
Paulo Marconi - An Author - Polite and caring
Marcus – TV producer – A nice chap

Scene one: In the studio corridor

Marcus: Hey Skye, good to see you. I wondered where you were

Skye: I was in Make-up.

Marcus: Is it going to be a good show tonight?

Skye: Well, there maybe some fireworks.

Marcus: Why is that?

Skye: The researchers have booked Sasha who thinks she is the country's top influencer and Jasmine – Who also thinks she is the country's top influencer.

Marcus: Hmm that should be interesting.

Skye: Maybe

Marcus: And you have the Author, Paulo Marconi, of course.

Skye:Yes, he wants to promote his new book, How To Survive In Marriage.

Marcus: Ah yes, I read it recently. It was quite good.

Skye: It may have been even better if he were not divorced.

Marcus: Do you know if the coffee machine is fixed yet?

Skye: I didn't even know it was broken

Marcus: Well it wasn't broken as such but it was lying face down on the floor last time I saw it. I just wondered if they'd fixed it back on the wall yet.

Skye: It fell over?

Marcus: Pushed over - by that rock band that were appearing last night

Skye: I heard they created quite a stir.

Marcus: It wasn't really their fault

Skye: Why not

Marcus: Their manager told them to do it

Skye: Stupid thing to do

Marcus: Trying to get them publicity

Sky: What kind of publicity is that? We can push a coffee machine over.

Marcus:Beats me

Skye: Me too.

Marcus: Have any of the guests arrived yet?

Skye: Yes, Sasha is here. I just sent her along to make-up

Marcus: Ok, I am going to see if the coffee machine is upright, catch you later.

Skye: See ya.

******************************************

Scene Two: The Make-up Room

Sasha: Hello, I suppose this is the make-up room

Jo: Hi, It is indeed, Please come in.

Sasha: Is there room for both of us? Quite pokey isn't it.

Jo: Oh it's not so bad, we manage.

Sasha: I am hardly used to just managing

Jo: Hmm, ok, but let's get started on your make-up. I have a list here, and you are?

Sasha: I am what?

Jo: Sorry, I meant what is your name?

Sasha: I beg your pardon. Did you just ask me my name?

Jo: Yes, I have to check it off the list.

Sasha: I can't believe you ask that. Do you really not know who I am?

Jo: No, sorry, I don't

Sasha: You must have spent the last two years living in a cave!

Jo: No, I live in a bedsit in Islington

Sasha: Unbelievable! I AM SASHA, that's who.

Jo: Ok, ok. Let me look at the list. Err Sasha who?

Sasha: (indignant) Sasha who!!! Sasha who!!!!

Jo: Yes, Sasha who?

Sasha: It is just Sasha you moron. Just Sasha. Just the mention of that name will be enough for everyone in the word to know who I am. Except, apparently, for anyone living in a bedsit in Islington.

Jo: I am sorry that I do not recognise you. I didn't mean to be insulting, I just don't keep up with celebrity gossip

Sasha; What said about me is not gossip. It is just praise an admiration.

Jo: Ok, I see. And what is it exactly that you do?

Sasha: When you said you lived in Islington, is that the one in London or is there another one in Outer Mongolia?

Jo: It is the one in London

Sasha: So I assume you have no TV, no radio and no broadband and a phone that still plugs into the wall.

Jo: I have an iphone 12

Sasha: Maybe you should charge it up then

Jo: Can we please make a start on your make up?

Sasha: Yes, I suppose so, where shall I sit?

Jo: This seat over here would do just fine

Sasha: Would you mind cleaning it first please? I just paid 2000 pounds for this Brunello Cucinelli dress.

Jo: Really? My mum has one just like it. She bought it from Marks And Spencer's

***************************************

Scene Three: The studio foyer

Jasmine: Hey! - Hey! You there.( A slight pause) You with the funny jacket.

Paulo: Are you yelling at me?

Jasmine: I was not yelling. I was beckoning

Paulo: It sounded like yelling

Jasmine: Whatever, take this bag for me

Paulo: What! Why should I take your bag?

Jasmine: You are a porter or something aren't you?

Paulo: No! I am not a porter or something. I am Paulo Marconi and I am appearing on this show tonight.

Jasmine: Well so am I. And I am sure that they asked me first.

Paulo:It is no concern of mine who they asked first. What I do know is that I was not invited here to carry your bags

Jasmine: If you don't want to carry bags then you shouldn't dress like a porter

Paulo: I am not dressed like a porter

Jasmine: If I knew I was going to be on the show with a porter I would have declined

Paulo: For the last time, I am NOT a porter

Jasmine: What are you then?

Paulo: I am a writer?

Jasmine: What do you write?

Paulo: I write books. I am an author.

Jasmine:(incredulously) Books!

Paulo: Yes books.

Jasmine: I had no idea anybody read books any more

Paulo: I assure you they do

Jasmine:Which platform do you write on?

Paulo: I write in my office, not at a train station

Jasmine; (Fake shouting) Hello! Hello! Can anyone here tell me which century we are in

Paulo: You may think books are old fashioned but they are still read by many people. So what do you do?

Jasmine: I am famous.

Paulo: Not so famous that I recognise you.

Jasmine:Your loss.

Paulo: I think not. What are you famous for?

Jasmine: That's a first. Nobody has ever asked me before what I am famous for.

Paulo: Really?

Jasmine. Yes, really, they just know.

Paulo: Well I am sorry I don't.

Jasmine: I am an influencer?

Paulo: And what do you influence?

Jasmine: I influence people, and tell them what to buy and what to wear

Paulo: And you make money from that?

Jasmine: Yes, a lot of money actually,which is why I can afford to wear this Brunello Cucinelli dress.

Paulo: That name means nothing to me.

Jasmine: What are your books about?

Paulo: My most recent one is called: How To Survive Marriage

Jasmine: Really? And how are you surviving your marriage?

Paulo: I'm not, I'm divorced.

Jasmine: Must be a good book then.

********************************

Scene Four: The Studio Corridor

Skye: ( Loudly) Hey! Marco. Wait! I need a word with you.

(short pause)

Marco: What is it Skye? I need to get up in the control room.

Skye: Catastrophe!

Marco: Tell me. What's happened:

Skye; We have a problem with Sasha and Jasmine

Marco: What's the problem?

Skye: They are both wearing the same dress.

Marco: And they are angry about it?

Skye: No! They don't know it yet but I have seen them both.

Marco: Is it a big problem then?

Skye: YES! They will go berserk when they find out.

Marco: What are you going to do then?

Skye (surprised) What am I going to do?? It's not my problem Marco. It is the stations problem

Marco: Do we have another dress they can wear?

Skye: No way! For one, neither of them would wear a dress we gave them and for two, neither one would change and leave the other in the original dress.

Marco: What can we do then?

Skye: I have no idea but one thing is for sure, neither of them will go on national television like that

Marco: Well we definitely can't cancel the show, it's going out live.

Skye: And we can't do it with just one guest

Marco: Absolutely not.

Skye: Is there anyone we could call at the last minute to stand in?

Marco: Nobody that I can think of.

Skye: We have to think of something

Marco: Could you go and have a word with them both – see how they react?

Skye: They won't do it Marco, I promise you.

Marco: You could at least try

Skye: Ok, I'll try. Sasha is in make -up and Jasmine is with the author chappie.

Marco: Make sure you don't lose him as well

Skye: See you later, I'll keep you posted.

**********************************

Scene Five: The Make-up Room

Skye: Hi Sasha, Hi Jo

Jo: Hello Skye, almost done here

Sasha: I should think so too. It's taken long enough

Jo: It is your own fault. You wouldn't keep still. Kept fiddling with your phone.

Sasha: I was not fiddling with my phone. Somebody as important as me has a string of messages and they have to be answered.

Jo: When I looked down you were playing Candy Crush

Sasha: Don't be so stupid.

Skye: Sasha, I just dropped by because I need a word with you.

Sasha: Really

Skye: Yes, we have a bit of a situation

Sasha: Which is?

Sky: Well, you know we also have Jasmine on the show tonight?

Sasha: Yes I had heard that. I think she does a bit of influencing.

Skye: Quite a lot actually. Anyway, that is not the point. The point is, she is wearing the same dress as you.

Sasha: WHAT! Impossible. This is a Brunello Cucinelli

Jo ( very quietly) Marks and Spencer

Skye: Wherever it came from, she has the same one

Jo: ( Even quieter) Marks and Spenser are doing well.

Sasha: Get out of my way girl

Jo: Oh, sorry for living

Skye: What are you doing Sasha

Sasha: I am going to find that amateur and rip the dress off her

Skye: No, no you can't do that we have a show to do

Sasha: You think I can't do that? Just watch me

Skye. Wait - wait, please wait.........

********************************

Scene Six: The Foyer

Jasmine: Well you may be a famous author that I have never heard of but I can't stand chatting here all day, I have to get to make-up

Paulo: I shall see you on the set, and I can find out what an exciting life you have influencing people.

Jasmine: And maybe the presenter can tell us if anyone has actually read you book yet.

Paulo: What exciting lives we both lead.

There is a loud shriek as Sasha appears

Sasha: Hey you! You girl! What the hell do you think you are doing by wearing that dress?

Jasmine: I could say the same for you. Now get that dress off right now.

Sasha: I will not but you will and if you don't take it off I'll rip it off.

Jasmine: Like Hell you will

Paulo: Ladies, ladies surely there is no need for this violence

Jasmine: There is every need. All this woman does is copy me

Sasha: COPY YOU!!! You are the one that hangs on my coat tails trying to steal my success

Paulo: Surely we can find a way to settle this amicably?

Skye: ( Out of breath) Oh there you both are. Can we please sort this out? We have a show to do

Sasha: Yes we can sort this out alright. I'm leaving

Skye: No, no. please don't leave, we have to do the show, I can find you another dress.

Sasha: Find her another dress. A cheap one, it will suit her

Jasmine: Why you, you....

Paulo: No don't hit her.

Skye: Too late!

Jasmine: Oww ow. Let go of my hair.

Sasha: You just bruised my face, I'll make you pay for that

Paulo: Ladies, ladies please stop fighting.

Skye: SECURITY! SECURITY – In the Foyer, quickly!

******************************
